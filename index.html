About Us

DCAS is an accredited English language school based in Dublin, Ireland. Established in 2007, DCAS offers General English and IELTS Preparation courses. DCAS is an accredited English language school located in the heart of Dublin City Centre. We offer short term and long-term courses to meet your individual learning needs. Recognised by the Department of Education and Skills and the Department of Justice and Equality, DCAS offers programmes to international students in Ireland and around the world.
